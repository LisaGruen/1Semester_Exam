Task description

The task was to create an information video and a website based on a brochure from the Copenhagen Municipality of “How to bike in Copenhagen” for foreigners. We defined the target group as young adults who just arrived to the capital like for example exchange students or tourists. Refering to this we tried to create a entertaining and fun video that informs and adverts our website in the same time. On the website we focused on the “how to bike” part of the brochure with useful tips. Furthermore, we integrated useful links for renting bikes or bike tours through the city and an inspiring gallery with images from bike lovers in Copenhagen.

What I learned

I could attend and participate very late in the process and group work Therefore, I took more an external role and could test and judge the project with a fresh eye. Here I learned more about group work and how to give constructive feedback.

What I would change today?

When I was coming to the group the other group members already defined their roles. Again I was not part of the coding part. This I would change today, because I want to get better and this requires practice.
